AI Face Recognition Guide

Face Detection

The AI automatically detects faces in uploaded photos and video frames with 99.7% accuracy.

Face Encoding

Each face is converted into a unique mathematical representation (128-point encoding).

Similarity Matching

The system compares encodings to find matches across the entire database.

Understanding Match Results
Confidence Score Interpretation Action Recommended
90-100% Very High Confidence Match Immediate investigation recommended
70-89% High Confidence Match Review by officer recommended
50-69% Moderate Confidence Manual verification required
Below 50% Low Confidence Likely different person
Best Practices for AI Matching
  • Use clear, front-facing photos when registering cases
  • Ensure good lighting in uploaded images
  • Upload multiple angles when available
  • For CCTV footage, extract clear frames before upload
  • Regularly update the face database with new cases
